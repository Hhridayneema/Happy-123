🎭 Our Story

Happy Creations was started with the motive to provide customers affordable and good quality clothes — both to rent and buy.

Founded by Neha Neema in Pune, Happy Creations has been serving families, schools, and event organizers for over 3 years with a wide range of costumes and ethnic wear.

From kids fancy dress competitions to Navratri celebrations — we have something for every occasion and every budget!
